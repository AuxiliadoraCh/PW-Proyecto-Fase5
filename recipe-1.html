Garlic Parmesan Fries

Crispy on the outside, tender and fluffy on the inside, and topped with the perfect combination of savory garlic and tangy Parmesan, this is an easy upscale for fries.

by Eren

Exclusive

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredientes

  • Potatoes: 3 large
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Parmesan: 50g, grated

Pasos para la receta

  1. Prep Spuds: Scrub potatoes & cut into fries. Soak them in cold water (this removes starches).
  2. Season and Cook: Toss fries in oil & seasoning and bake or air fry per recipe below.
  3. Toss in Garlic Parm: Melt butter & garlic and toss with fries and parm.
